Todd Graham

My name is Todd Graham and I am currently working on Master's in Computer Science at Portland State University. I have a Bachelor's of Science in Biology and Mathematics from University of Portland. Currently, I am working as a Graduate Research Assistant focusing on using Data Science to analyze United States election data. Additionally, I manage a research laboratory at Oregon Health & Science University that is studying pre-hospital hemorrhage control and resuscitation for traumatic injuries. I am very interested in the intersection of computer science and medical research; as my career progresses I'd like to continue to contribute and innovate in both of the fields.
